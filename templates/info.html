About Navigate For Rate

"Navigate For Rate" is a fullstack application that allows users to create and save driven miles that are reinbursable by their employer.
Users can reccount a driven route along with a purpose for their commute and get the accurate mileage, mapview, and expenses owed in a simple application.
Traveling salesfolk, engineers, scientists, medical staff, and other professionals who utilize their personal vehicle for work will find value in this application.
Just setup an account, create your route, and we handle the rest.
(Reinbursment rates are based off newly innacted July 2022 IRS rates. See link for additional details: IRS Details)
